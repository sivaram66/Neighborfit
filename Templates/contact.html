Quick answers to common questions about NeighborFit.

How does NeighborFit work?

NeighborFit uses advanced algorithms to analyze your preferences and match you with neighborhoods that fit your lifestyle, budget, and priorities.

Is NeighborFit free to use?

Yes! Our basic neighborhood matching service is completely free. We also offer premium features for more detailed insights and analysis.

How accurate are your recommendations?

Our recommendations are based on comprehensive data analysis and have a 95% user satisfaction rate. We continuously improve our algorithms based on user feedback.

Do you cover my city?

We currently cover over 500 neighborhoods across major US cities, with new areas being added regularly. Contact us to check if your area is covered.
